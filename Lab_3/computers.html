How to build a budget PC

There are so many different ways to build a PC that fits individual needs. Do you want a power house video rendering PC? A strictly gaming PC? A streaming machine? Or just a PC to browse the web? There are so many applications that picking the correct "components" is as easy as trying to talk normally while eating peanut butter.
