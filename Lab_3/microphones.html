"On-a-Budget" Desk Microphone

Basic on desk microphone, can match color schemes and get the job done on a budget

Pros: Low cost, plug-n-play, long USB cable

Cons: Audio quality is bad, can head typing, omni-directional, can be bumped

Rating: 4/10

"On-a-Budget" Microphone + Boom Arm + Pop filter

Basic cardiod microphone with on-board auto adjustment nobs with a included boom arm, pop filter.

Pros: Plug-n-play, better audio quality due to boom arm, pop filter included

Cons: cardioid pick-up pattern is very limiting in direction microphone can be placed, non-refutable brand

Rating: 6/10

High End Microphone (my current microphone)

Small, high quality on-desk microphone with a supercardioid pickup pattern with higher quality recording quality in a small, compant package

Pros: good price to value, small size, mountable on boom arm with included thread adapter

Cons: no manual adjustments (all software adjustments in capture software), no on-board mute button

Rating: 9/10

Overkill Microphone

Studio level microphone trusted by professionals and streamers. Highest audio quality possible with no professional audio equipment.

Pros: high quality and trust brand, on-board manual audio adjustments

Cons: not budget friendly, needs extra equipment to interface between microphone and PC

Rating: 9/10
