Purpose of this site

Welcome to the first iteration of of this 'How-To' site that is geared for those who are new to streaming or those who want to see what new innovations there are in the realm of streamers. As the ability to stream becomes easier and easier for anyone to particpate in, I have compiled a range of information to help further make the search for equipment and components easier for research and find.
