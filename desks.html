"On-a-Budget" Desk (my current desk)

Good mix between usability and cost, biggest complaint: not much desk space and not as stable as I would espect from the structure

Pros: Low cost, asthetic, shelf space, foot rest

Cons: Wobbly, small desk space, small sitting area, chips easily

Rating: 8/10

"On-a-Budget" Gaming Desk

Good mix of asthetics, "gamer" functionality, budget friendly "gamer" desk

Pros: Low cost for a "gaming" desk, asthetic, quality of life chacteristics built in

Cons: Small desk area, no storage space, quality may vary

Rating: 7/10

Budget Standing Desk

Reasonably priced sit-to-stand desk, automatic raise/lower, comes in different colors, holds decent amount of weight

Pros: good price range, decent tabletop size, different colors, auto raise and lower

Cons: does not remember heights, weighs 100lbs alone

Rating: 7/10

Overkill Desk

Made from real wood in different finishes, one of the best desks you could get for a wide array of hobbys

Pros: Large desktop size, real wood, holds up to 300lbs

Cons: not budget friendly

Rating: 9/10
